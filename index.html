FAQs

Find answers to your most pressing questions about MindSpace and how it can help you.

Is MindSpace free?

MindSpace offers a free beta version for users to explore its features. After the beta, there will be affordable subscription options. We aim to keep mental wellness accessible for all students.

Is my data safe?

Your privacy is our top priority. MindSpace employs advanced encryption and data protection measures. We do not share your information with third parties.

How does it work?

MindSpace guides you through short, effective mindfulness exercises tailored to your needs. Our app utilizes cognitive behavioral therapy techniques to help manage stress and anxiety. You'll find tools like mood tracking and breathing exercises to support your mental health.

Can I trust it?

Daniel Berhanu, a licensed therapist with a deep understanding of student challenges, is the founder of an innovative app designed to deliver reliable support in a secure environment. Trust is fundamental to our mission, and we are dedicated to upholding it at all times.

How can it help?

MindSpace helps you develop coping strategies for academic stress and social anxiety. With regular use, you can improve your mental resilience and emotional well-being. Our exercises are designed to fit into your busy student life.
